# Privacy Policy — KeepYouSafe

**Last updated:** July 16, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how **KeepYouSafe** ("Bot," "we," "us") processes data when you use the Bot on a Discord server. We are committed to protecting user privacy and only process the minimum data necessary for the Bot to function.

## 1. Data We Process

When you use the Bot's commands, the following data may be processed **temporarily**, only for the duration needed to handle your request:

- Your **Discord user ID** and the **server (guild) ID** where the command was run — sent automatically by Discord to the Bot as part of the standard slash command mechanism, as with any Discord bot.
- The **URL** you submit with the `/download` command.
- The **media file** (video/photo) downloaded from that URL, temporarily stored on the server running the Bot before being sent to your Discord channel.

We do **not** request or store additional personal information such as your real name, email address, phone number, or payment details, as this Bot is free and has no account system.

## 2. How We Use This Data

The data above is used **solely** to carry out the command you requested, specifically to:

- Fetch and deliver media from the Instagram/TikTok URL you provided.
- Return the result (embed, file attachment, or download link) to the Discord channel where the command was run.

## 3. Data Storage & Retention

- **No persistent database.** KeepYouSafe does not keep a history of commands, submitted URLs, or media files.
- Downloaded media files are stored temporarily on the Bot's local server storage only while the download and delivery process is running, and are **automatically deleted** once delivery completes (or if an error occurs).
- If a file is too large to send directly on Discord, it is temporarily uploaded to a third-party file hosting service with a limited retention period (1–24 hours), and a link is shared with you. After that retention period expires, the file is automatically deleted from that third-party service. We have no control over the file once it has been uploaded there.

## 4. Sharing Data with Third Parties

To process a `/download` request, the Bot sends the URL you submitted to third-party services/APIs in order to retrieve media data and metadata (such as captions and thumbnails) from Instagram/TikTok, as well as to a temporary file hosting service for large files.

- We do **not sell** your data to anyone.
- We do **not use** your data for advertising or marketing purposes.
- Data sent to these third parties is subject to their respective privacy policies, which are outside our control.

## 5. Cookies & Tracking

KeepYouSafe is a Discord bot and **does not use cookies**, trackers, or third-party analytics technologies to monitor user behavior.

## 6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data processed while the Bot is running (for example, automatic deletion of temporary files). However, as with any online service, we cannot guarantee absolute security for data transmitted over the internet or through third-party services.

## 7. Children's Privacy

This Bot follows the age requirements set by the [Discord Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms). We do not knowingly target or collect data from children under Discord's minimum required age.

## 8. Your Rights

Because KeepYouSafe does not permanently store personal data, there is generally no personal data held in our systems that would need to be deleted. However, if you have any questions or concerns about your data, please reach out via the contact below.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes take effect once published. Continued use of the Bot after changes are published constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
